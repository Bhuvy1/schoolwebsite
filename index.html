This is our Campus Life

Vision : Ambitus World School will endeavour to be an institution that will stay true to the expectations, evolution and experiences of the new era by enabling community-based, globally-relevant learning that values each individual and encourages the young to make their own place in the world.







Mission : To inspire, educate and enlighten every individual in an environment that shall be the foundation to a future of opportunities and the launchpad to aspirations. We will work towards nurturing individuals who will arise as the enablers of 'Leadership for a Better World'.
